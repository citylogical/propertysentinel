# Product Context — Claude Code Reference

## Property Sentinel
propertysentinel.io — Chicago property intelligence and compliance monitoring.
Target customers: STR operators, landlords, property managers, hard money lenders,
property tax attorneys.

## Pricing (STR segment)
Flat building fee + $2/unit above 10 units, capped at $400/month:
- 1-6 units: $35/mo
- 7-20 units: $60/mo base + $2/unit above 10
- 21-50 units: $100/mo base + $2/unit above 10
- 51-100 units: $150/mo base + $2/unit above 10
- 101+ units: $400/mo cap

## Know Your Building — Feature Card Structure
Four feature groups in this order:
1. 24/7 Monitoring
   - 311 complaints, violations, and permits as they happen
   - Stop-work orders and administrative hearing activity
   - BACP license and registration expirations
2. Short-Term Rental Tracking
   - Airbnb and VRBO listing activity
   - Prohibited Buildings List and Restricted Residential Zone encroachment
   - Prioritized STR 311 complaints
3. Property Tax Intelligence
   - Uniformity comparables for Class 2 and 3
   - Cap rate analysis for commercial
   - Benchmarking CCAO assumptions against actuals
4. Comprehensive Property Report
   - City records, building characteristics, and ownership history
   - Valuation extrapolation from assessments, permit activity, and recent transactions
   - Permit history with open/closed status and inspection outcomes

## Chicago STR Licensing
- SHUOL: operator-level license, $250/year, required for 2+ units
- Unit registration: $125/unit/year, required per listed unit
- Prohibited Buildings List: ~2,411 buildings where STR is prohibited
- RRZ: 52 precincts where STR density is capped
